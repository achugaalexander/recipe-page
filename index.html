Indomie Instant Noodles

Description

Indomie Instant Noodles is a very quick food produced to satisfy hunger with minimal effort and time, hence the name, "Instant". It is synonymous to the Japanese food, Rahmen.

Cooking Time

10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • Water
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Parboil noodles in water for a minute to remove preservatives.
  2. Fry fresh pepper and onion in a pot of very little oil to bring out the taste, and remove them from the pot.
  3. Break egg in a plate, add little salt, spice, and some of the fried pepper and onion.
  4. Fry egg in that same pot of little oil to give it better taste.
  5. Add parboiled indomie to that pot and add little water, the remaining spices, pepper and onion and add grinded pepper to give it even better taste and color.
